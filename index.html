Passkey User Study

Project Summary

Recently, major companies have started rolling out passwordless authentication (also known as passkeys) to their users. Passkeys are being implemented as an alternative to traditional password-based authentication. In this work we (a group of researchers at Cornell Tech & Cornell University) are investigating the usability of passkeys through a user study with a number of human participants.
